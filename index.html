About Me

Hello! My name is Sean, and welcome to my portfolio page! I graduated with a B.S. in Biochemistry/Chemistry from University of California, San Diego in 2019. In my free time, I like to take photographs and edit them. In addition, I like keeping up with tech news, such as computer component releases, smartphone news, and etc. I am a native English speaker and am fluent in speaking Mandarin Chinese. I am conversational in Japanese, and can understand conversational Cantonese, but am not able to speak it.
